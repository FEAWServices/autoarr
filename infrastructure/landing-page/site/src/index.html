Why AutoArr?

Your media stack works hard. AutoArr makes it work smarter.

Configuration Intelligence

AI-powered auditing identifies misconfigurations, suggests optimizations, and explains the "why" behind every recommendation. No more guessing at settings.

Automatic Recovery

Failed downloads? AutoArr detects failures, tries alternative indexers, adjusts quality settings, and keeps your queue moving - all without intervention.

Natural Language Requests

"Download the latest season of Shogun" or "Get Dune Part Two in 4K". AutoArr understands what you want and routes it to the right service automatically.

Real-Time Monitoring

Live WebSocket updates show exactly what's happening across your entire stack. Activity logs, health scores, and instant notifications.

Privacy First

100% self-hosted. Your data never leaves your network. Open source (GPL-3.0) with optional premium cloud features that connect outbound - no firewall holes needed.

Docker Native

One container. All your services connected via MCP (Model Context Protocol). Deploy on any Docker host, Synology NAS, or Kubernetes cluster.

AutoArr acts as an intelligent orchestration layer for your existing stack.
